Spaghet

Instructions

Ingredients:

  • Spaghet Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Spaghet Toucher

Directions:

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al-dente
  2. Add oil and minced garlic into a pan and let saut√© until fragrant
  3. Pour and heat up the tomato sauce in the pan on medium-high heat until simmering
  4. Add salt and oregano to taste
  5. Stir in a tablespoon of pasta water and add the spaghet noodles into the sauce
  6. Make sure someone touches the spaghetti officially transforming the dish into spaghet
